I saw BIGFOOT!!!

MeIsZwi - 2/5/25
One day when I was walking home from my schooling, eating a bag of sunflower seeds, I saw it! A large silhouette was walking across the path. I stopped in my tracks and said, “What is that thing, and what is it doing here?”

Then, the bigfoot looked straight at me and said “Excuse me? My pronouns are they/them/he/him!”

I walked up to him and said, “I’m sorry dude. It’s just, you know, you’re bigfoot.”

“Yeah… I get that a lot. It’s ok,” he said. We both decided to go to an arcade. We both ate pizza and played games. It was a fun day. Afterwards, he went back to the forest. I used to be a skeptic because I didn’t trust other people’s accounts, but now, I’m a full believer.

So… uhh… by for now!
